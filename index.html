The Sydney Experience

6 Days • Premium Arrival Program

$1499

The solution to arriving overwhelmed, disconnected, and unsure where to start. Everything is curated for ambitious travellers who want Sydney done properly.

Day 1 — Arrival & Rooftop Welcome

Settle into Paddington’s newest luxury stay, complete your bank, TFN and SIM setup, meet your group over welcome drinks, and ease into Sydney from a rooftop overlooking the city.

Day 2 — Sydney Icons & Harbour Party

Explore The Rocks, wander Circular Quay, discover hidden laneways, then board an evening harbour boat party with cocktails and skyline views.

Day 3 — Bondi Beach & Surf Experience

Start with beachside coffee and pastries, take the Bondi coastal walk, then join your optional curated surf lesson before sunset drinks.

Day 4 — Blue Mountains Escape

A full-day curated escape through iconic lookouts, waterfalls, walking trails and scenic mountain villages.

Day 5 — Taronga Zoo Experience

Catch the harbour ferry to Taronga Zoo, spend the day exploring with incredible harbour views, then return for an elevated evening social.

Day 6 — Final Dinner & Next Chapter Planning

Personalised onward travel planning, East Coast guidance, resume support, and a final curated farewell dinner.
